Baxter

Life is busy, so I don't think I'll write a lot about the map. This is Baxter, a control point map for Team Fortress 2 taking place in Baxter, Maine inside of a nuclear power plant. Please consider voting for it on the Steam Workshop below. This has been my labor of love for the past three years, finished in 2024.
